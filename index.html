About

The Story

One day like any other, you go to sleep… but when you wake up, you’re no longer in your room.

You've been pulled into a strange place known as the Digital World, a realm built from data, circuits, and fragments of forgotten technology.
Parts of the system are corrupted, and dangerous programs roam freely through the code.

Your journey begins in CPU Town, a small city located deep inside the system’s core. There you’ll meet Byte-Boy and other NPCs who are trying to repair and maintain what’s left of the digital world. But something is spreading through the system. A powerful corruption known as ByteRot is slowly infecting everything, and if no one stops it, the entire digital world may collapse.
